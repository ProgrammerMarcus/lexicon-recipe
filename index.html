RAVIOLI: The most comfortable food?

In a test consisting of 100 dishes, it was found that ravioli was the most comfortable. In the test, 78% of participants choose to use ravioli as a pillow. Comparatively, only 9% were willing to use pancakes. Scientists are still baffled.

What are the ingredients of a ravioli?

In attempt to determine the reason behind the overwhelming ravioli preference, scientist used reverse engineering and managed to find the following ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive Olive
  • Whole Milk Ricotta
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Fresh Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

How does on make a ravioli?

After reverse engineering the ravioli, scientists managed to construct the following recipe for creating ravioli:

  1. Make Pasta Dough
  2. Make Ravioli Filling
  3. Roll Pasta Dough Into Sheets
  4. Assemble & Cut Ravioli
  5. Store it
